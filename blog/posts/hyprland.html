Hyprland Setup

2026-05-29

After using Linux Mint XFCE for a few months and enjoying it, I decided to switch to Arch Linux for its rolling release model and lightweight design.

The installation went surprisingly smoothly, and I got Arch up and running without too many issues. After that, I wanted to try a tiling window manager, and I ended up choosing Hyprland.

Unlike traditional stacking window managers like macOS, GNOME, or Windows, Hyprland automatically tiles windows as you open them. This makes multitasking much more efficient, especially for keeping multiple apps visible at once.

As a student taking advanced CS classes, this setup helps me stay organized and productive while working on projects and studying.
