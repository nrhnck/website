Xteink X3 Review

2026-05-29

The Xteink X3 is a unusual little device. When I bring it around in school, often my friends will ask "What is that?" My best explanation is, "its like a mini kindle". And it is essentially, it has an eink screen like the kindle, it reads books like a kindle does, but it doesn't come preinstalled with amazon bloat.

Out of the box, kindles come preinstalled with tons of useless things, such as ads, and the kindle store (which yea, some people could use) I have a kindle, but I jailbreaked it w/koreader because I was tired of this bloat.

I wanted to read more, and my kindle was kinda hard to bring around everywhere with me, which is why I was looking into a pocket ereader. Initiilay, I was looking into the boox palma or hisense touch, these are chinise ereaders that are around the size of a phone and have andriod on them. While these devices are really cool, the price of 250-300 bucks was way out of my budget, plus, importing on ali express was extra pricey. I then came across a video by youtube James Scholz, showing off the Xteink X4, which was this niche little ereader without a touchscreen and used buttons. I looked a little more into the company, and saw that they had just released the X3, a smaller version of the ereader. I asked my parents for the device, and they gave it to me on my birthday.

Now, this device is so nice. I thought it wouldnt be super comfy to read on, considering its size, but it is actually extremely comfortable to read on, and I usually read for around 1-3 hours on it daily. I really like changing the orientation to landscape mode, its super comfy to read on it like that when I am in bed, or on the couch. On stock firmware, there were many times where my books would get formatted weirdly, so I looked into it. Many reddit users suggested Crosspoint, which is a open source firmware for the device. I flashed it, and was instantly suprised on how much better the reading expeirience was. The only downside to crosspoint was the wallpapers, sometimes my wallpaper would glitch out, or just not show at all.

In conclusion, this device is fucking awesome, I would deffinetly recommend it!
