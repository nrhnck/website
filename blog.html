Hyprland setup! 5/29/26

After using Linux Mint XFCE for a few months and loving it, I wanted to switch to arch (btw) because of the rolling release model and the lightweight anti-bloat nature of it. Somehow, the installation process went smoothly, and I installed Arch (btw). I then wanted to try a tiling window manager, and I landed on Hyprland. Unlink regular stacking window managers such as Macos, GNOME, or Windows, everytime you open a window on Hyrpland, it automatically tiles, which is super nice for student use, as you can easily have a web browser, document, and whatever else you may need open at the same time. As a cs student, and just a highschool student taking advanced classes, this allows me to easily multitask and overall be more productive.
